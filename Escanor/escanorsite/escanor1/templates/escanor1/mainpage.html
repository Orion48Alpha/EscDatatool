Escanor Data Cleaning

Less clutter. More cleaning.

Escanor is built so Business Analyists and Engineers can download and begin cleaninging their data in minutes.
It is built on the belief that 80% of the data cleaning process comes from about 20% of functions made available by a product. Escanor brings data alterations in termonology anyone can understand and an interface that anyone can use.
Escanor is designed to give BAs autonomy and give Engineers more time to do valueable work.

    Features

  • Tools to clean, transform and analyse your data
  • Ability to access files both on your pc and on the cloud
  • Export the result of your transformations
  • Take the python and pyspark code used to generate the output so you can productionise the transformations
    Design Concepts

  • If you need a tutorial, the software has become to complex
  • You should be helped by escanor, not dependant on it.
  • Focus on the simple tasks and execute them perfectly.
